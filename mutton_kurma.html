Mutton Korma

Korma is an ultimate delight for meat lovers. This korma recipe makes for a great dinner party dish apart from being a mouth-watering staple one during festivals like Eid. It is an easy mutton recipe that you can prepare at home in just about an hour.

prep time

2 hrs.

cook time

30 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Heat the oil in a deep dish, add the finely sliced Onions and lightly fry it.

step 2

Add the mutton, curd, salt, water, coriander powder, red chilli powder and stir for a few minutes.

step 3

Now add the garlic, ginger, cloves, green cardamom and the cinnamon sticks and mix well.

step 4

Cover it and cook on a slow fire for about 30 mins, then add the fried onions and stir again.

step 5

Cover and cook for an hour or till done.

step 6

Garnish with thin deep fried onion rings and serve hot.

ingredients

1/2 bowl oil

1 Onion(Sliced)

700 gms mutton

3-4 teaspoon Curd

1 bowl Water

1 teaspoon Salt

2 teaspoon Coriander Powder

1 teaspoon Red Chilli Powder

1 teaspoon Garlic(roughly grounded)

1 teaspoon Ginger(roughly grounded

3-4 Cloves

2-3 Whole Green Cardamom

2-3 Cinnamon Sticks

2 teaspoon Onions(fried)

tools

Medium-size cooker.

Tongs and a spider.

Ladle.
