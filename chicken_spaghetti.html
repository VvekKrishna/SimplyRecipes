Chicken Spaghetti

If you're looking for a way to jazz up your next spaghetti dinner, OR possibly looking for a creative way to use up some leftover or rotisserie chicken, you've come to the right place. This creamy, cheesy, chicken spaghetti is so incredibly flavorful, make-ahead and freezer-friendly, and your family will love it. Take a look at how to make it, read through my pro tips, storage information, and more below.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Boil the water in a dish, add 2tbsp oil and 1tsp salt then add 250g spaghetti and stir gently until all the strands sink beneath the water level and ensure that the strands are not stuck together.

step 2

Take 3tbsp olive oil into another dish, add 300g boneless chicken and cook until the chicken changes colour and turns white.

step 3

Add salt, 1tsp ginger powder, 1tsp garlic powder, 1/2tsp paprika powder, 1tsp red chilli flakes and mix it well.

step 4

After cooking it for 3-4 mins and add 1tsp oregano, 1/2 cup chopped onion, 3tbsp yellow capsicum, 3tbsp red capsicum, 3tbsp green capsicum and cook for 3-4 mins.

step 5

Add 1 cup pasta sauce, 4tbsp tomato sauce, 1tbsp sugar and cook for 10 mins on medium heat.

step 6

And finally add boiled spaghetti into the chicken mixture and mix well and cook for 5 mins and server hot.

ingredients

300g boneless chicken

3 tbsp olive oil

Salt to taste

1/2 tsp paprika powder

1 tsp ginger powder

1 tsp garlic powder

1 tsp red chili powder

1 tsp oregano

1 cup marinara sauce or pasta sauce

4 tbsp tomato sauce

3 tbsp yellow Capsicum

3 tbsp red Capsicum

3 tbsp green Capsicum

1/2 cup water

1 tbsp sugar
